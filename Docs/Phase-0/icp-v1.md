## 1. Ideal customer

Automation agencies managing between 5 and 30 active clients, primarily using n8n at the start.

## 2. Operational profile

These agencies build and maintain workflows for their clients involving:

- leads
- forms
- emails
- WhatsApp
- CRM
- billing
- data synchronization
- business notifications

## 3. Main pain points

- silent errors discovered too late
- loss of leads / orders / opportunities
- Slack spam with no ownership
- no centralized multi-client console
- difficulty proving service quality
- difficulty distinguishing noise vs. real incidents

## 4. Why they would pay

They pay to:

- detect issues faster
- deduplicate noise
- assign ownership clearly
- meet SLAs
- generate proof for their clients
- professionalize their agency offering

## 5. Why this segment first

- urgent pain
- direct business impact
- natural multi-tenant need
- n8n allows a simpler first integration
- potential to expand to Make / Zapier later

## 6. What the v1 ICP is not

We are not targeting in v1:

- complex enterprise companies
- generalist DevOps teams
- solo users
- traditional infrastructure monitoring
- agencies without critical workflows
- multi-tool players from day one

## 7. Simple positioning statement

Automation Incident OS helps n8n-first automation agencies detect, group, handle, and prove incidents across their multi-client workflows before they turn into invisible business losses.
